folding

i suppose i am a folder

one who folds

this was me folding on the river

i was 21 then...

youth and energy folding into myself. memories tucked and packed away in some attic of me.

is it a fold or roll?

brent seemed to think of it as a roll 6 years ago

i fold every day, even today...
i once folded the sun

and someone tripped over it...
i tried to apologize for leaving the sun there...
but it was them who almost crushed my poor sun
lying there, folded
trying to guard itself against falling feet

feet seeking to fold the sun and the earth beneath their soles

globetrotters trotting for the sake of trotting

nooks...

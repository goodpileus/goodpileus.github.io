Principles

  • Empathize with all creatures

  • Work tirelessly to imagine a new space (there will be no escape hatch until we build it)

  • Pride your hand over everything else, work to remember it when you realize you have forgotten it

  • Treat the website as if it were a building, consider: the warmth, the "site," the material, the local weather, the various organisms that grace its halls

  • Tread slowly
